Kasutera

Description

A kind of honey cake from Japan.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Soften the butter.
  2. Add honey into butter and mix evenly.
  3. Add egg into the ingredients.
  4. Add flour into the ingredients
  5. Pour mixed ingredients into mold and send into pre heated oven.
