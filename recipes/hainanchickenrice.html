Hainan Chicken Rice

Description

Base is simple long rice mixed with chicken oil. Serve along with tender chicken and special chili sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • Long rice
  • Green Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chili
  • Soy sauce
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Cook the Chiken thigh with green onion and garlic in low heat.
  2. Cook the rice.
  3. Prepare sauce, mix chili, lemon and soy sauce.
  4. Cut the cooked chiken thigh into smll portions.
